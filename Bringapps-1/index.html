WHY CHOOSE US?

Webschool, S.L. is an ASP (Application Solution Provider) for language teaching organisations, which has been developing and distributing educational resources based on new technology and providing advice on the implementation of blended learning teaching methods since 1999.

  • We develop and distribute tools which help and support off-line, on-line and blended learning teaching processes.
  • We develop tools for the administration of blended and distance learning.
  • We provide training and support for teachers, tutors and administrators.
  • We provide commercial and marketing support on behalf of our clients to potential end users.

More than 300 language centres, in 4 continents, have used Webschool services!
